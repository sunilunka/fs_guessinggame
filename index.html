YOUR FLEET IS UNDER ATTACK!

This is a classic hot and cold game with a maritime feel...

A rogue submarine is terrorizing your fleet!

You are on an anti-submarine helicopter. You have 100 sonobuoys in the water, but due to a equipment glitch can only ping each one once

Each time you ping a sonobuoy it will give you an indicaton of whether you are getting hotter (closer to the sub) or colder (further away).

The catch...each time you aren't successful, the submarine could damage another ship...15 ships damaged and the mission is failed.

Once you have the 'hottest' sonobuoy in contact: DROP THE TORPEDO TO WIN!

Using the hint button calls the alert Anti-submarine aicraft, and gives you the closest sonobuoy to attack, but you get six ships damaged in the process. It takes a while for the aircraft to get to the fleet. Use it wisely.

Sink the Submarine!
